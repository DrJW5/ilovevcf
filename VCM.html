vCard Manager

vCard (.vcf files) is a standard to store and exchange a variety of information about individuals and other entities, such as names, phone numbers, emails, etc. It is commonly used by "contacts" apps on phones or laptops.
This tool helps you visualize and edit your contacts and their infos as a large array.
You should be able to export your contacts as a .vcf file from the settings of your contact app. You can then open and edit this file on this webpage. Once you are done with your edits, you can save a new .vcf file and import it in any compatible contact app.
